A Shared Open Core for BIRD Implementations

Eclipse Free BIRD Tools provides an open source, vendor-neutral core for implementing BIRD’s data models and transformation rules—so you don’t have to start from scratch. By leveraging this common foundation, banks and vendors can focus their resources on delivering superior value—such as enhanced integrations, refined user interfaces, robust audit controls, or streamlined XBRL generation and submission—instead of reinventing the wheel.

With a shared open core at its heart, this approach significantly reduces duplicated effort, ensuring that testing, maintenance, and improvements happen in one centralized place. The result is a more reliable, maintainable, and easily extensible platform that lets you devote your efforts where they matter most: refining your competitive differentiators and meeting the specific needs of your customers.

Eclipse Free BIRD Tools is governed by the trusted Eclipse Foundation and is fully available on GitHub. It’s built on well-established frameworks like Python Django, understood by millions of developers worldwide and easily integrated with AI-driven solutions. By representing BIRD artifacts in a familiar language, you gain immediate alignment with AI tools, simplifying development, testing, and ongoing innovation.

At the core lies PyBIRD AI, a key component that expresses BIRD’s concepts in a Python Django-based environment—an approach designed to accelerate understanding, customization, and progress. With Eclipse Free BIRD Tools, you’re empowered to harness the future of BIRD implementations while staying focused on the features and enhancements that set your offerings apart.
